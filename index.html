Vincent is a crime predicting artificial intelligence (AI) that works by learning crime trends from historical data. The trends are generalizations of crime patterns, and are the consequence of many crime trends in the last couple of years. Larger trends get picked up, but more importantly nuanced trends are also accounted for when generating a crime forecast. By having a crime forecast, Station Sergeants will have an easier time allocating specialized crime fighting units. This makes the process of crime fighting in HPD a more proactive one.
