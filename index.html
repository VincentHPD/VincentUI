Vincent’s purpose is to augment the Houston Police Department (HPD) so city resources are allocated more efficiently. This will save the City of Houston money while making it a safer city for its citizens. Vincent is a crime predicting artificial intelligence (AI), it works by learning crime trends from historical data. The trends are generalizations of crime patterns, and are the consequence of many crime trends in the years prior. Larger trends get picked up, but more importantly nuanced trends are also accounted for when generating a crime forecast. By having a crime forecast, Station Sergeants will have an easier time allocating specialized crime fighting units. This makes the process of crime fighting in HPD a more proactive one.
